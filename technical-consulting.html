Technical Consulting

Pragmatic advisory for modern technology and delivery.

Thompson Consulting works with leadership teams to clarify where technology needs to go, what needs to change in delivery, and how to sequence that work in a way that your organization can actually execute.

What We Focus On

Technical consulting engagements are typically centered around three connected areas:

  • • Technology strategy and roadmapping aligned to the business
  • • Data, analytics, and AI readiness across platforms and practices
  • • Software delivery and DevOps optimization for predictable outcomes

The goal is not to deliver a static slide deck—it’s to help you make a small number of well-informed decisions that move the organization forward.

How We Engage

Every engagement starts with your context: existing platforms, current teams, vendor relationships, constraints, and time horizons. From there, we structure the work so it’s clear what happens first, what can wait, and what success should look like.

  • • Focused discovery sessions with key stakeholders
  • • Clear articulation of current state and pain points
  • • A practical roadmap with options and tradeoffs
Typical Outcomes

Technical consulting work is designed to leave you with artifacts and decisions you can actually use—whether or not there is a long-term engagement afterward.

  • • A right-sized technology roadmap tied to business priorities
  • • Clear guidance on where to invest, consolidate, or simplify
  • • Recommendations for improving delivery, collaboration, and flow

Depending on your needs, that may mean a short, concentrated advisory engagement or a longer-term fractional CTO-style relationship.
