Executive Recruiting

Targeted search for the leaders who shape your trajectory.

Thompson Consulting provides boutique executive and senior-level recruiting services for organizations that need high-impact leaders—and a search partner who understands both the technical and human sides of the role.

What We Focus On

We specialize in roles where technology, operations, and leadership intersect. That often includes executive and senior positions such as:

  • • CTO, VP of Engineering, and Head of Technology
  • • Data, Analytics, and AI leadership roles
  • • Senior delivery and transformation leaders

Every search is built around your organization’s context—your existing team, your roadmap, and the type of leader you need to make meaningful progress.

How the Search Works

Rather than running a volume-driven process, we focus on a lean, deliberate search built around:

  • • A clear definition of success for the role
  • • A targeted candidate universe, not mass outreach
  • • Structured evaluation of both capability and fit

Throughout the process, you get direct communication, honest feedback, and a partner who respects your time and your team.

What You Can Expect

Executive recruiting engagements are typically structured around a clearly defined search scope, agreed-upon milestones, and regular touchpoints with your leadership team.

If you’re considering a critical leadership hire, we’re happy to talk through what a focused search would look like and whether we’re the right fit for your organization.

Share a bit about the role you’re considering, your organization, and your timeline. We’ll follow up to discuss whether a dedicated search engagement makes sense and what the next steps could be.
